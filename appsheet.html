APPSHEET FAQs

Duplicate records on the appsheet.

step1

Try to find out what might be the cause of the duplicate records, i.e different phone numbers.

Step2

Find the person who registered the customer and find more details about the customer.

Step3

Send an email to It.support @suncuture.com for it to be sorted.

My appsheet does not sync.

step1

Clear cache and try to sync the appsheet again.

Step2

Check on your Network and restart your laptop

step3

If the above steps does not sort the issue,Screenshot the error, and send it to IT.SUPPORT@SUNCUTURE.COM for the issue to be sorted.

User profile cannot load.

NB, This is normally caused by slow Network

step1

Check your Network Connection, preferably if using bundles change to WIFI.

Step2

Clear all browsing data/cache.

Step3

Restart your laptop/phone.

Step4

Try login in again,and if the issue persist,Kindly send an email to it.support@sunculture.com

I cannot dispatch from the Appsheet.

Send Email to It.support@sunculture.com
